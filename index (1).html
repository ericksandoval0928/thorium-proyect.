Torio - Thorium final project

The project consists of us, the three-tenths, making a giant periodic table. we were given. Professor Blanca gave each of us an element and we have to make a cube of the element we were given.

project summary

1003 and other classes want to make a giant periodic table. Professor Blanca gave each of us an element, and groups were formed. Each group bought the colored cardboard assigned to them, then we made the cube template and wrote the information on it in English.
